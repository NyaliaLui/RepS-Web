RepS is a replay organizer that will look at all SC2 replays in a zip archive and sort them. The creator, Nyalia "Noticals" Lui, has been a SC2 fan since 2011 but has grown tired searching through large replay packs for the matches and series he wants to watch. This tool is designed to make replay organization easier for both streamers and viewers alike.
